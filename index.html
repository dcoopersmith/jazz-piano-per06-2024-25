About Jazz Piano

Jazz is a uniquely American form of music. It was created by African-American musicians in the city of New Orleans, Louisiana. Jazz piano players use their left hand to create rhythm and their right hand to improvise melodies. Here is some jazz history.

Famous Jazz Player - Oscar Peterson

One of the Best - Oscar Peterson

Oscar Peterson is considered by many to be one of the greatest jazz piano players of all time. His career lasted more than 60 years. And he performed in more than 1000 concerts. Some called him the "King of inside swing".
